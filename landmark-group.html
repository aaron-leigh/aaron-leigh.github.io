User Experience Design

Differentiating in a dense, competitive landscape

Landmark Buddy - The brief

Design a product through which a customer will be able to explore in-storepromotions and will be able to purchase 3 or lesser number of products withminimal to none store personnel intervention.

Customer Journey

Mapping customer stages to product features.
Defining of customer journey and its various stages for a new and a returning customer. This exercise led us to the features that need to be given to the users and at which part of their journey.
