Cyacomb

Cyacomb is an innovative, internationally scaling digital forensics start-up.

Client

Cyacomb

Role

Visual Design, Motion Graphics, UX Design

Overview

Branding innovative digital products

Challenge

A key aspect of Cyacomb's strategy to expand international growth was a full rebrand, undertaken by branding agency Mr. President. However, constantly evolving functionality and strategy required expansion of this design system.

Solution

My role was to build out the brand and design system created by Mr. President into new assets for Cyacomb's digital products, as new use cases for new users developed.

Role

Visual Design, Motion Graphics, UX Design

Putting the brand in motion

Logo Motion Graphics

A key approach to lead generation for Cyacomb's products is video content on social media, though their branded intro sequence was a static graphic.

I developed the company's new logos into dynamic motion graphics with a consistent identity across sub-brands, capturing attention on crowded social feeds.

Developing feature icons

Icon Design Workshop

Building components and patterns from the refreshed design system into new icons was key to communicating evolving technical capabilities and use cases.

Developing assets for comms and documentation required constant cross-functional collaboration.

To develop new icons visualising rapid digital triage, for example, I ran a workshop with the VP Product and VP Engineering to ensure accurate communication of product features.

Icon Design

Icons were crafted to visualise complex technical processes for expert specialist audiences, such as this icon representing file hashing.

Design for lead generation

Central to promoting such innovative products is highlighting value offered in key use cases for a range of specific end users.

Animation Storyboarding Workshop

To develop an animation demonstrating digital triage products, I pitched storyboard iterations to the VP Sales and Head of Business Development, incorporating feedback to ensure the needs of key stakeholders are met.

Delivered Animation

The final animation was tailored to EMEA and US markets respectively.

Implementation

Animations were used at various expos and conferences to drive lead generation. Feedback collected through interaction with users honed future communication.

Responsive design for the web

Responsive Website Animations

A key project was the development of a new website. To develop dynamic content, I reworked existing product feature visualisations into device-responsive motion graphics.

Professional comms to the last detail

Email Signature

An issue posed in more direct comms was inconsistency in the appearance of email signatures across different providers.

Through prototyping and testing with team members to troubleshoot issues, I developed a customisable, provider-agnostic template, with documentation.

Final delivery

During my time at the company, I delivered leading content for the launch of Cyacomb Safety, and assets I developed are in use generating leads for Cyacomb Forensics products around the world.

Cyacomb recently closed a £6.5m funding round to continue scaling into new markets.
